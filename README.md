Business Recommendations:
Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can help improve workforce management and business performance:

i) Strengthen Hiring Strategy:
Hiring declined significantly in 2023 compared to previous years. HR teams should review recruitment channels and workforce requirements to ensure adequate staffing levels.

ii) Focus on Employee Development:
Although most employees meet performance expectations, employees in the "Needs Improvement" and "PIP" categories should receive additional training and mentoring programs.

iii) Optimize Workforce Distribution:
The Production department accounts for the majority of employees. Management should regularly evaluate staffing levels to avoid over-dependence on a single department.

iv)  Improve Employee Engagement:
The average engagement score is moderate (2.94). Conducting employee feedback surveys and recognition programs may help improve engagement levels.

v) Enhance Employee Satisfaction:
The average satisfaction score is 3.02, indicating opportunities for improvement. HR can focus on career development, employee benefits, and workplace culture initiatives.

vi) Monitor Training Effectiveness:
The organization invested ₹17.65M in employee training. Management should track training outcomes to ensure a positive return on investment and improved employee performance.
